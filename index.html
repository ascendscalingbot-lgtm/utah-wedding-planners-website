Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your proposal

How far in advance should I contact Utah Proposal Planners?

We recommend 4-8 weeks for best vendor and location availability, but we can accommodate rush planning when needed. The more notice, the more options we have to make your proposal truly special.

Can you help me choose the perfect engagement ring?

While we don't sell rings, we partner with trusted local jewelers and can coordinate ring pickup as part of your proposal plan. We're happy to recommend our favorite Utah jewelers.

What if I only need day-of coordination?

Absolutely! We offer day-of coordination packages for those who have their plan but want professional execution. This ensures everything runs smoothly without the stress.

Are your services suitable for same-sex couples?

Yes! We're honored to plan proposals for all couples. Love is love, and we're here to make your moment perfect regardless of who you are or who you love.

What's included in your planning packages?

Our packages include unlimited consultation calls, vendor coordination, timeline creation, location scouting, permit handling, and day-of coordination. Photography can be added to any package.

Do you provide services outside of Utah?

We specialize in Utah proposals and know the best locations intimately. For destination proposals, we can coordinate with trusted partners in select locations — reach out to discuss your vision.
