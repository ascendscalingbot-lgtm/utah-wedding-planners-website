Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I contact Utah Proposal Planners to plan my proposal?

We recommend 4-8 weeks for best vendor and location availability, but we can accommodate rush planning when needed.

Can Utah Proposal Planners help me choose the perfect engagement ring?

While we don't sell rings, we partner with trusted local jewelers and can coordinate ring pickup as part of your proposal plan.

What if I only need help with the day-of coordination for my proposal?

Absolutely! We offer day-of coordination packages for those who have their plan but want professional execution.

Are your services suitable for same-sex couples?

Yes! We're honored to plan proposals for all couples. Love is love, and we're here to make your moment perfect.
